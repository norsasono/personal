Megan Michelle

----- Megan Michelle is a conscious American clothing company offering minimalist sophistication. The company capitalizes upon the skills, work ethic and positivity of refugees who have resettled within the United States of America to create unique garments for men and women. Sourcing only natural fabrics, Megan Michelle clothing is the natural alternative to synthetic modern fashion.
